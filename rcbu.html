RCBU

Rotaract Club of Bombay Uptown is a youth run organization part of the International Rotaract movement.

This app is an introductory informatory public relations targeted with features to reach them or register with them.

Features of the app:

  1. Gives a complete overview of the club
  2. Easy Navigation
  3. Ability to Register
  4. Push Notification for events

Build using Android Studio and Firebase

The app is avaliable on play store and has over 100 downloads and 4.9 rating
