NewspapAR

Although technology has increased rapidly and has infiltrated every common items, one item that has not changed much are Newspapers. They help us gather information easily and effectively. But their effectiveness in conveying information is limited by the fact that they cannot show moving images or content.

The main objective of the project was increase interactivity and allow newspapers to convey more than just static content.

Thus I developed an application that superimposes a video onto a particular image in newspaper helping the reader gain more information regarding the news at hand, like a video showing how the goal was scored rather than just a picture of the player

Build using Unity 3D and Vuforia Engine
